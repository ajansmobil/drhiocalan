Our Mission

Providing the Best Orthopedic Service to Our Patients

Increasing quality of life through modern medicine and patient-centered service.

What is Our Mission?

As Op. Dr. Halil İbrahim Öçalan our mission is to provide our patients with the highest quality orthopedic healthcare and improve their quality of life.

Specialized in orthopedics, we follow current scientific developments. We view each patient as an individual value, centering their needs at every stage of treatment process.

Core Mission Objectives

Patient-Centeredness

Every patient is an individual value. We consider our patients' needs and expectations at every stage of treatment process.

  • Individual Approach: Each patient evaluated specifically; personalized treatment planning
  • Open Communication:Transparent communication with patients; questions answered
  • Choice Freedom:Patients informed about treatment options
  • Patient Rights:All patient rights respected, privacy protected

Patient-centeredness is foundation of treatment success.

Scientific Development

We follow current scientific developments in diagnosis and treatment. Latest findings from international literature integrated into our protocols.

  • Continuous Education:Our team attends international congresses, learns new techniques
  • Academic Publications:We produce scientific studies, publish papers
  • Clinical Guidelines:Follow and apply international guidelines
  • Research:Active participation in clinical research

Scientific development raises treatment quality.

Intervention Quality

In every surgical procedure we use minimally invasive techniques, modern imaging and safe anesthesia. We pay attention to infection control.

  • Minimally Invasive Surgery:Maximum visualization with minimum damage
  • Modern Technology:Latest technology equipment used
  • Anesthesia Safety:Specialist anesthesiologists monitor patients
  • Infection Control:International sterilization standards applied

Intervention quality relates to patient safety.

Medical Excellence

We keep our knowledge updated through continuous development programs. We follow new technologies.

  • Specialist Training:Clinical team participates in education programs
  • Technology Tracking:New devices evaluated
  • Experience Sharing:Case reviews conducted
  • Performance Measurement:EQUIPMENT performance measured regularly

Medical excellence achieved through continuous improvement.

Patient Safety

To maintain patient safety at highest level we apply risk management protocols.

  • Error Prevention:Systemic error prevention mechanisms
  • Identity Verification:Patient identity verified before procedures
  • Surgical Checklist:WHO safety checklist applied
  • Safety Culture:Learning-oriented approach for errors

Patient safety is indispensable part of our mission.

Open Communication

We establish transparent communication with patients. We provide information about treatment options and risks.

  • Informing:Options explained before treatment
  • Question-Answer:Patient questions answered clearly
  • Communication Channels:Channels kept open post-appointment
  • Digital Access:Pain portal allows access to results online

Open communication is foundation of patient-doctor trust.

Service Areas

  • Arthroscopy surgery (shoulder, knee, ankle)
  • Prosthesis surgery (hip and knee replacement)
  • Sports surgery and injury treatment
  • Trauma surgery (fractures, dislocations, ligament injuries)
  • Hand surgery and microsurgery
  • Diabetic foot treatment
  • Limb lengthening surgery
  • Rehabilitation programs

Our service areas expanded according to patient needs.

Organizational Values

  • Ethics:We adhere to medical ethical rules. We respect patient rights, protect privacy.
  • Respect:Serve every patient equally
  • Responsibility:We know our responsibilities, strive for patient trust
  • Scientific Approach:Prefer scientific evidence in decisions
  • Innovation:Use modern medical technologies with innovation drive
  • Collaboration:Multidisciplinary approach; holistic solutions
  • Sustainability:Eco-friendly applications, efficient resource use

Our organizational values reflect in every stage of patient experience.

Service Quality

  • Modern operating centers; international sterilization standards
  • 24-hour emergency service
  • Multidisciplinary team work
  • Advanced imaging (MRI, CT)
  • Fast recovery programs (ERAS)
  • Patient follow-up system; online result inquiry

Our service quality periodically inspected by international accreditation organizations.

Community Contribution

  • Awareness activities; spine health education programs
  • Free health screenings; mobile clinics
  • Educational programs; collaboration with physical therapy centers
  • Campaigns; World Spine Day events
  • Assistance programs; discounted treatment for low-income patients
  • Research support; guidance for university projects

Community contribution is integral part of our mission.
