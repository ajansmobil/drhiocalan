Sports Surgery

As Op. Dr. Halil İbrahim Öçalan, we provide expert sports surgery services in Gaziantep. Sports surgery deals with diagnosis, treatment and rehabilitation of muscle, ligament, tendon, cartilage and joint injuries occurring during sports or physical activities.

Common Injuries

  • Anterior cruciate ligament (ACL) injuries
  • Meniscus tears
  • Rotator cuff tears
  • Shoulder dislocations
  • Ankle ligament injuries
  • Muscle and tendon tears

Diagnostic Methods

Detailed physical examination, special orthopedic tests (Lachman, pivot shift, McMurray), MRI imaging, X-ray and ultrasound are used for accurate diagnosis of injuries.

Treatment Options

  • Conservative treatment: Rest, ice, compression, physiotherapy, PRP injections
  • Surgical treatment: Arthroscopic methods (closed), ACL reconstruction, meniscus repair, rotator cuff repair

ACL Reconstruction

Common in sports requiring sudden direction changes like football and basketball. The torn ligament is reconstructed using graft taken from patient's own tendon. Post-surgery 6-9 month rehabilitation program is applied after arthroscopic surgery.

Meniscus Injuries

C-shaped cartilage structures that act as shock absorbers in the knee. Depending on tear type and location, either repair or partial meniscectomy is performed. Both procedures are done arthroscopically.

Rehabilitation

Early motion after surgery, muscle strengthening, proprioception training and functional exercise programs enable safe return to sports. Full sports activity is achieved 6-9 months after ACL reconstruction.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is surgery necessary for ACL tear? - Yes for patients with instability and active athletes
  • Can meniscus tear heal on its own? - Small outer tears may heal, inner area requires surgery
  • When can I return to sports? - 4-6 weeks after partial resection, 6-9 months after ACL reconstruction
